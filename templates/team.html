Dr. Rahul Johari

Project Mentor & Faculty In-charge

Dr. Rahul Johari is an Associate Professor at the University School of Automation & Robotics, GGSIPU. With over 220 publications and multiple patents, he leads research in IoT, cybersecurity, and cloud computing. He is the founder of the SWINGER Lab and has served as an ACM Eminent Speaker. As Faculty In-Charge, he mentors the Student Registration System, Student Feedback System and Event - Hours Evaluation System projects, promoting transparency and technical excellence. His guidance ensures secure, student-friendly digital platforms. He is also the Associate Director (Student Welfare) and actively supports student innovation through clubs and campus initiatives.
